Introduction

The Cardano Fund Governance system provides a secure and transparent mechanism for managing organizational or community funds. It ensures that all deposits are controlled, approved by designated officials, and only released when conditions are met.

üí° Key Point

Funds remain locked in the smart contract until approval conditions are satisfied, preventing unauthorized access or misuse.

Getting Started

1

Wallet Setup

Use a Cardano-compatible wallet (e.g., Lace, Nami, Eternl). Ensure sufficient ADA for deposits and transaction fees.

2

Understand Roles

Familiarize yourself with system roles: Fund Owner, Officials (approvers), and Participants.

Deposit

1

Create Deposit Proposal

The Fund Owner specifies the deposit amount and a description of the purpose. This proposal will be subject to official review.

2

Deposit funds using your wallet. The smart contract locks the ADA until official approval is granted.

Approvals

1

Official Review

Designated officials examine the deposit proposal. Each official either approves or rejects the proposal.

2

Once the required number of officials approve, the deposit qualifies for release. Rejected deposits may be refunded.

1

FAQ

Q1

What happens if a proposal is rejected?

The deposited funds can be refunded automatically to participants‚Äô wallets once the rejection is processed on-chain.

Q2

Can the Fund Owner change the recipient?

No. The recipient is defined in the deposit proposal. Officials can only approve or reject proposals.

Q3

How can I verify approvals?

All approvals are recorded on-chain and can be checked via your wallet or Cardano blockchain explorer.
