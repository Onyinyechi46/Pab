Complete Governance Workflow

Follow the interactive journey from locking funds to final release or refund. Each step is secured by blockchain technology and multi-signature protocols.

STEP 1

Lock Funds

Deposit your ADA into a secure smart contract with multi-signature governance rules. This creates an immutable vault that requires official approvals for any withdrawal.

Blockchain Security

Funds protected by Cardano's Plutus smart contracts

Multi-Signature Setup

Define officials and approval thresholds

Time-Locked

Set deadlines for approval collection

Refund Protection

Automatic refund if approvals not met

STEP 2

Collect Approvals

Authorized officials review the fund release request and submit their approvals on-chain. Each signature is cryptographically verified and permanently recorded.

Digital Signatures

Cryptographic proof of official approval

Real-Time Tracking

Monitor approval progress live on-chain

Immutable Record

All approvals permanently timestamped

Threshold Verification

Smart contract validates approval count

STEP 3

Release or Refund

Based on the approval status and deadline, funds are either released to the recipient or refunded to the owner. The smart contract handles this automatically.

Release Funds

When sufficient approvals are collected before the deadline, funds are released to the designated recipient address.

  • Threshold met
  • Deadline not passed
  • Instant transfer
  • Transaction recorded

Refund Owner

If approvals aren't collected by the deadline, the original owner can claim a full refund of the locked funds.

  • Deadline passed
  • Threshold not met
  • Owner verification
  • Full fund recovery

Workflow Complete!

The governance process has been completed successfully. All transactions are permanently recorded on the Cardano blockchain for complete transparency and auditability.
